ABOUT ME: INTRODUCTION

STEM Student & Drawer

I see life as a whirlwind adventure, a chaotic masterpiece painted with mystery and endless possibilities. As a STEM student with a passion for drawing, I navigate between logic and creativity, sketching dreams as much as I solve equations. But beyond my own journey, my true goal is to shape an education that ignites others fueling their curiosity, their passion, their fire. Just like a single spark can set the night ablaze, I want to inspire others to chase knowledge fearlessly, to create, to explore, to dream.
